ABOUT COMPANY

We believe in sustainable energy practices

Cirqulus Solutions Private Limited is a technology-enabled waste management company working at the intersection of sustainability and compliance. We connect waste generators, collectors, recyclers, and regulators on a unified platform — making circular flows transparent, traceable, and commercially viable.

Founded in Raipur, Chhattisgarh, we bring deep operational expertise across multiple waste streams and regulatory frameworks. Our team combines environmental science, logistics intelligence, and software to deliver end-to-end solutions that actually work on the ground.

We believe a circular economy isn't just a concept — it's a business model. And we're building the infrastructure to make it real, at scale, across India.
